# Privacy Policy — Vim What?

**Last updated: 2026-03-07**

## Summary

Vim What? does not collect, transmit, or share any user data. Everything stays on your device.

## Data Collection

Vim What? collects **no data**. We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, crash reporters, or any form of telemetry.

## Data Storage

The following preferences are stored **locally in your browser** using the browser's built-in `localStorage` API:

- Which keys you have marked as learned
- Your custom key mappings and notes
- Your selected keyboard layout (Qwerty, Colemak, etc.)
- Your current lesson level

This data never leaves your device. It is not sent to any server.

## Permissions

The extension requests the `storage` permission solely to persist the above preferences between browser sessions using `localStorage`. No data is ever written to or read from any remote storage.

## Third-Party Services

Vim What? makes no external network requests. No third-party SDKs, APIs, or services are used.
